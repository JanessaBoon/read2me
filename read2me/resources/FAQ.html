Welcome to the Read2Me! F.A.Q


Q: What is Read2Me!
R: Read2Me! is an open-source text-to-speech software, with which a computer reads text aloud.

Q: How do I use it?
R: Read2Me! had been developed to be a simple to use as possible.
1. SELECT a text from any application (i.e. Firefox, Internet Explorer, Word, Acrobat Reader...)
2. COPY it (right click-> copy or CONTROL+C or menu Edit-> Copy)
3. PASTE it in Read2Me! Clic on the text area and use the keyboard shortcut CONTROL+V
4. Clic on the PLAY button to start reading.

Q: How do I navigate through the text?
R: You can either use the navigation buttons or use the keyboard shortcuts.
NB: The keyboard shortcuts work only once you click the play button.

Q: What are the keyboard shortcuts?
R: to paste copied text : CTRL+V
: SPACE BAR (start reading and turn OFF the text edition)
: SPACE BAR (pause the reading)
: UP ARROW (go back one paragraph)
: LEFT ARROW (go back one sentence)
: RIGHT ARROW (go to next sentence)
: DOWN ARROW (go to next paragraph)
: RETURN (stop reading and turn ON the text edition)

Q: What is ?
R: this icon informs you whether or not you can edit the text. Once you start reading the text, you cannot edit it.
You need to click if you want to edit the text.

Q: How do I export the text to an audio file?
R: Clic on . Chose the destination folder, the file name and its format (MP3 or WAV) and clic save!
You can now put the file on your audio player or burn it on a CD.

Q: How do I change voice?
R: Click either on Preferences or on Current Voice in the menu toolbar. The "Preferences" window appears.
Chose a voice from the drop down menu. Save.

Q: How do I change the text size/color/font/background color and the window color?
R: Click on Preferences in the menu toolbar. The "Preferences" window appears.
Pick the colors/font/size that you want. Save.

Q: Read2Me! does not read an abbreviation or a word appropriately? What can I do?
R: Click on Preferences in the menu toolbar. The "Preferences" window appears.
Click on manage in front of "Abbreviations". Then follow the instructions.
Save your changes before closing the file!!!

Q: How do I add new voices?
R: Follow this link. Download SpeechSDK51.exe. Install it. Close Read2Me! and the next time you start it, new voices will appear in the voice list.
